Subcommittee Charters
Pre-Clerkship Curriculum Subcommittee (PCCS)
25 voting · 5 non-voting seats · oversees Phase 1
Design, implementation, integration, and evaluation of the pre-clerkship phase, aligning foundational science and early clinical learning with the Medical Education Program Objectives.
View Download PDF
Clinical Curriculum Subcommittee (CCS)
24 voting · 6 non-voting seats · oversees the clinical phases
Design, implementation, and evaluation of clinical education — required clerkships, acting internships, and clinical learning experiences — including comparability across sites and assessment standards.
View Download PDF
Curriculum Integration Subcommittee (CIS)
19 voting · 4 non-voting seats · horizontal & vertical integration
Alignment and sequencing of content across phases — curriculum mapping, longitudinal threads, Areas of Concentration, and integration of foundational and clinical sciences.
View Download PDF
Assessment and Evaluation Subcommittee (AES)
11 voting · 1 non-voting seat · assessment & program evaluation
Assessment strategy and program evaluation across the curriculum — alignment of assessment methods with objectives and use of evaluation data for continuous improvement.
